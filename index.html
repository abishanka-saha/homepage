About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher at TU Eindhoven working in cryptography. My research interests include post-quantum cryptography, symmetric key cryptography, multi-party computation, and provable security. I have worked extensively on Mirror Theory and indifferentiability frameworks and am now transitioning toward quantum-safe constructions and distributed key generation protocols.
